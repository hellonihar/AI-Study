# 6. Adding AI Flavor to Enterprise Experience

Bridging traditional enterprise engineering roles to modern AI system development.

## Why This Section

You already have years of enterprise experience — architecture, backend engineering, QA, DevOps, data pipelines, governance, and stakeholder management. These skills are directly transferable to AI development. The concepts, mindset, and processes you already know map cleanly onto AI system design — the difference is the terminology, the tools, and a few new abstractions.

This section takes each traditional enterprise role and shows:
- **What you already know** that applies directly
- **What's new** — the AI-specific concepts and tools
- **How to translate** your existing expertise into AI-role skills
