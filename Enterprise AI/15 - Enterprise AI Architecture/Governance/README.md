# Governance

Governance frameworks and oversight mechanisms for enterprise AI.

## Topics
- AI review board: charter, membership, approval processes, escalation
- Model risk classification: tier 1–3 based on impact, autonomy, and data sensitivity
- Compliance mapping: EU AI Act requirements, GDPR, CCPA, sector-specific regulations
- Audit readiness: immutable logs, model version history, decision records
- Bias and fairness: audit methodology, disparate impact analysis, mitigation
- Transparency: model cards, system cards, capability disclosures
- Vendor risk management: third-party model assessment, contractual safeguards
