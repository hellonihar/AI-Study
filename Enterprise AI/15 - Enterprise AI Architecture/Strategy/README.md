# Strategy

Strategic decision-making for enterprise AI adoption and investment.

## Topics
- Build vs. buy analysis: managed APIs vs. self-hosted vs. open-source
- Model sourcing: OpenAI, Anthropic, open-weight models (Llama, Mistral), fine-tuned variants
- Vendor evaluation: reliability, pricing, data residency, SLAs, roadmap
- ROI modeling: cost-benefit analysis, productivity gains, error reduction
- Organizational design: CoE model, federated vs. centralized AI teams
- Talent strategy: hiring, upskilling, roles and career paths
- Roadmap planning: capability maturity model, phased rollout, risk mitigation
