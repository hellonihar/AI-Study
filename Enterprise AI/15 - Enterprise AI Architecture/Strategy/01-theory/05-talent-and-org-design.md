# Talent & Organizational Design

## AI Team Structure

### Foundational Roles

| Role | Skills | Seniority |
|------|--------|-----------|
| ML Engineer | Model serving, deployment, monitoring | Senior |
| ML Platform Engineer | Infrastructure, automation, tooling | Senior/Staff |
| Prompt Engineer | Prompt design, evaluation, iteration | Mid/Senior |
| Data Engineer | Data pipelines, feature engineering | Senior |
| AI Product Manager | Use case definition, prioritization | Senior |
| AI Ethicist | Bias audit, fairness, compliance | Senior |

### Advanced Roles (Level 3+)

| Role | Skills | Seniority |
|------|--------|-----------|
| AI Architect | System design, capacity planning | Staff/Principal |
| Research Scientist | Custom model development | PhD+ |
| Fine-tuning Engineer | PEFT, RLHF, data curation | Senior |
| AI Security Engineer | Guardrails, red teaming | Senior |
| AI Governance Lead | Policy, compliance, risk | Director |

## Organizational Models

| Model | Best For | Team Size | Coordination |
|-------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Centralized AI CoE | Starting out | 3-5 | Low |
| Federated AI Teams | Scaling across units | 5-15 per unit | Medium |
| Platform + Embedded | Mature org | 10-20 platform + per-team | High |

## Career Paths

| Track | Junior | Mid | Senior | Staff | Principal |
|-------|--------|-----|--------|-------|-----------|
| ML Engineer | Implement features | Own model lifecycle | Design systems | Platform architecture | Org-wide strategy |
| ML Platform | Support tooling | Build pipelines | Platform design | Multi-team platform | Industry standards |
| AI Product | User stories | Feature ownership | Use case strategy | Multi-product strategy | AI transformation |

## Talent Strategy

### Hiring
- Hire for ML fundamentals, not specific frameworks
- Prioritize engineering over research for most roles
- Consider contractors for spike capacity
- Build internal AI literacy program for non-ML roles

### Upskilling
- ML bootcamp for software engineers (8-12 weeks)
- Prompt engineering training for all product teams
- AI ethics training for all builders
- External conferences and workshops budget

### Retention
- Access to GPU compute for side projects
- Paper-reading clubs and research time allocation
- Conference speaking opportunities
- Defined career progression with AI specialization tracks
