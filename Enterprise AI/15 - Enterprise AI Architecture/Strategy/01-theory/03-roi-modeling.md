# ROI Modeling for Enterprise AI

## ROI Framework

### Cost-Benefit Matrix

| Category | Cost | Benefit |
|----------|------|---------|
| Infrastructure | GPU, hosting, networking | Lower cost per task |
| Development | Engineering, ML talent | Automation of manual work |
| Operations | Monitoring, support, training | Quality improvement |
| Compliance | Audit, documentation, legal | Risk reduction |

### Quantified Benefits

| Use Case | Metric | Typical Improvement |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Customer support | Cost per ticket | 30-50% reduction |
| Code generation | Developer productivity | 20-40% faster |
| Document processing | Processing time | 80-90% faster |
| Content generation | Time to create | 50-70% faster |
| Data analysis | Insights per week | 2-5x more |

## ROI Calculation Example

### Customer Support Chatbot

**Costs (Monthly)**:
- API inference (500K req, 1K tokens avg): $300
- Development (6 months, 2 engineers): $40K total
- Operations: $2K/mo
- Year 1 total: $40K + ($2.3K × 12) = $67,600

**Benefits (Monthly)**:
- Tickets handled by AI (40% of 50K tickets): 20K tickets
- Cost per human ticket: $8
- Savings: 20K × $8 = $160K/mo
- Year 1 savings: $1.92M

**ROI**: ($1.92M - $67.6K) / $67.6K = 2,741%
